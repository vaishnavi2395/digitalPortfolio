Projects

Pizza Deliziosa

A fusion of culinary delight and timeless elegance inspired by the Neoclassical era. Drawing the symmetrical balance of the period, the box features clean lines, muted tones, and subtle architectural motifs. Grecian columns frame the edges, adding a touch of grandeur to the packaging. The color palette, inspired by ancient marbles and muted earth tones, imparts a sense of sophistication. It's a refined presentation that transforms every pizza Box into a culinary masterpiece, elevating the dining experience with a touch of Neoclassical grace.

Arôme

Inspired by the opulent aesthetics of the 18th century, the packaging boasts intricate filigree patterns and delicate pastel hues, reminiscent of Rococo artistry. The box structure mirrors the era's fluid forms, exuding a sense of grace and elegance. Gilded gold accents add a touch of luxury, while asymmetrical details invite exploration from every angle. The fragrance name is presented in ornate, scripted fonts, paying homage to the handwritten scripts of the Rococo period.

Berrilicious

When I was creating the packaging, it was necessary to emphasize the bright taste of soda with the colorful packaging, contrasting and saturated colors. Stylized fruits, as if casually drawn by a child's hand, are a hint of the playfulness of taste, its richness and spontaneity. The logo was designed based on a font with a slight stylization of soda bubbles. The whole project is based on vivid images of fruits, which reflects the company's concept of fun, kindness and freedom.

La Délicatesse

Every project starts with story. I was inspired by the Post-Impressionism time period to design this, and awaken a sense of nostalgia for convivial dinners with family and friends. Inspired by artists like Van Gogh and Cézanne, our menu design captures the essence of swirling brushstrokes and bold hues. The typography echoes the organic flow of post-impressionist lettering, creating a harmonious fusion of art and information. Each section mirrors the diverse and eclectic nature of a gallery, enticing patrons to savor not just the flavors but also the visual feast that our menu promises.
